Barcelona

04/06/2017

Barcelona, an attractive city which is famous for its high-level football team and the masterpieces ofAntoni Gaudí i Cornet.

  Sagrada Família

A place that you can not miss as a tourist in Barcelona. The sculpture on the wall outside of it is so delicate and lifelike, but what I appreciate the most is the structure inside, Gaudí made the light go throught the colorful stained glass from the cold colors to warm colors, which represent the birth of Jesus and the sufferings he went through.

You can buy the tickets on the official website of Sagrada Família to avoid queuing up for them at the entrance. And make sure that you choose the ticket with the Top View and the audioguide.

  SCasa Milà

This building is so famous, I'm sure that you've already seen some pictures of it even if you don't know its name before. To my surprise, there are still many residentes, so remember to be quite when passing by someones' houses.

  SCasa Batlló

A private house redesigned by Gaudí in 1904, operated by its owner as a museum now. It is located about 3 minuits' walk from SCasa Milà but much smaller that. The museum offers the electronic guide to every visier at the entrance. With the help of VR technology provided by the E-guide, we can discovery the entrie house with so many surprises and funs.
